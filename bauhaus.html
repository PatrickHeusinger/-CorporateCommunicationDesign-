Elena Reiniger - Corporate Communication Design

Biotech company BRAIN is located in a historic Bauhaus building from the 30ies. On the occasion of the 100th Bauhaus anniversary, the BRAIN technology campus has been transformed into a interdisciplinary event location. By combining science, arts, culture, music and dance, a various program was offered to visitors, neighbours, employees and clients. Core of the event was the video light projection Moving Mitosis by light artist Helga Griffiths which was projected on the outside walls of the building.
